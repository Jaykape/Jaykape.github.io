Sheaves of Modules

Def  An \(\mathcal{O}_{X}\)-module \(\mathcal{F}\) is  free  if it is isomorphic to some  \(\bigoplus\mathcal{O}_{X}\)
and is  locally free  if \(X\) can be covered by open sets \(U\) for which \(\left.\mathcal{F}\right|_U\) is a free \(\left.\mathcal{O}_{X}\right|_U\)-module.
